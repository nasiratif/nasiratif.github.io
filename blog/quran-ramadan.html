1 Ramaḍān '43 — Quick Benefit

How to complete the Qur'an by the end of Ramadan

Ramadan is here and because of this, this is an easy opportunity to complete the entire Qur'an in this blessed month.

The best part about this is that the steps are very easy, I will explain the steps in 3 methods:

Method 1: By the End of Ramadan

To complete the Qur'an by the end of Ramadan, read 4 pages of Qur'an after every obligatory Salat (prayer). This means that you will have read 20 pages of Qur'an after all 5 Salat was offered, and by doing this continuously every day in Ramadan, you will have completed the entire Qur'an by the end of Ramadan!

Method 2: In 12 Days

To complete the Qur'an in 12 days, read 10 pages of Qur'an after every obligatory Salat (prayer). This means that you will have read 50 pages of Qur'an after all 5 Salat was offered, and by doing this continuously every 12 days, you will have completed the entire Qur'an in 12 days!

Method 3: In 10 Days

To complete the Qur'an in 10 days, read 12 pages of Qur'an after every obligatory Salat (prayer). This means that you will have read 60 pages of Qur'an after all 5 Salat was offered, and by doing this continuously every 10 days, you will have completed the entire Qur'an in 10 days!

Conclusion

This Ramadan we can take this task as an easy opportunity to increase our knowledge and our Iman, May Allah (subhanahu wa ta'ala) give us a blessed Ramadan, and increase us in knowledge. (ameen)

May Allah bless you, and shukran.
