6 Ṣafar '44 — Quick Benefit

Reading Surah al-Kahf on Fridays

Friday is the best day of the week for Muslims, and one of it's benefits is to read Surah al-Kahf (18) on this day.

Abu Sa'id al-Khudri (رضي الله عنه) reported that the Prophet (ﷺ) said:

"Whoever recites Surat al-Kahf on Friday will have a light between this Friday and the next." (1)

Surah al-Kahf is a special chapter of the Qur'an, and if one memorizes the first ten verses of it, will be protected from Dajjal:

Abu Darda (رضي الله عنه) reported that the Prophet (ﷺ) said:

"Whoever memorizes ten verses from the beginning of Surah al-Kahf will be immune to the False Messiah." (2)

May Allah bless you, and shukran.

Sources :

1. al-Sunan al-Kubra 5856, graded Sahih according to al-Albani.

2. Sahih Muslim 809, graded Sahih according to Muslim.
