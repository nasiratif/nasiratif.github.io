29 Dhū al-Ḥijjah '43 — Quick Benefit

The Benefits of Reading Surah Al-Ikhlaas

The Qur'an is one of the most beneficial books to read, and includes some of the highest benefits, One of the benefits is that you recite Surah Al-Ikhlaas 10 times. This quick benefit will explain that here:

Benefits of Surah Al-Ikhlaas

Reciting Surah Al-Ikhlaas 10 times is a huge benefit. It will strengthen your piety and will please Allah. But one of the best benefits is that it will earn you a house in Jannah. Hadith is included to support this claim:

Mu'adh ibn Anas reported that the Prophet ﷺ said:

"Whoever recites the chapter ten times completely 'He is Allah, the One' (112:1), Allah will build a palace for him in Paradise." (1)

So the Prophet ﷺ tells us that Allah builds us a home in Paradise as a reward for reciting Surah-Al-Ikhlaas 10 times completely.

Conclusion

In this article you have learned the profound benefits of reading Surah Al-Ikhlaas, a great reward to get to Paradise forever.

May Allah bless you, and shukran.

Sources:

1. Musnad Ahmad 15183, graded Sahih according to Al-Albani
