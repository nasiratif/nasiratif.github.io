5 of 99 Names of Allah

Allah has 99 names but here I will be talking about just 5 of them, starting below:

Al-Haqq - The Truth

Ar-Rahman - The Most Merciful

Ar-Razzaq - The Provider

Al-Malik - The King

Al-Quddus - The Most Pure

As I mentioned eariler there are many more names that can be discussed but this is just 5 of them. I may continue at another time Insha-Allah.

Shukran.
