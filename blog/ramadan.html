Things to do in Ramadan

﷽

Ramadan is a great time of the year. There are many acts of worship able to be done on this blessed month. This article will dicuss some of the things you can and must do in Ramadan:

1. Fasting (obligatory)

Fasting in Ramadan is obligatory but is beneficial. In Ramadan, we fast from dawn to dusk every day continously in the month of Ramadan. After we apporach dusk, we eat Iftar (evening meal).

2. Read Qur'an

Reading Qur'an in the month of Ramadan has more benefits than reading it in normal days, and of course is more beloved to Allah (subhanahu wa ta'ala).

3. Purify Yourself

Purification is one of the virtues of Islam. It is always good to be in a clean state and is always appreciated by Allah (subhanahu wa ta'ala).

4. Recite Qur'an

Recite maybe a few pages of Qur'an. I recommend Surah Al-Ikhlaas, which has some of the highest benefits of all chapters in the Qur'an.

5. Finish the Qur'an

Read 4 pages of Qur'an after each prayer every day in Ramadan, and by the end of Ramadan you should have read the entirety of the Qur'an. Refer to this article for more information about this.

6. Go to the Masjid (mosque)

Going to the masjid during Ramadan is more benefitting than going in normal days. You'll usually find special Ramadan lectures which have amazing knowledge.

Conclusion

There are many things to do in the blessed month of Ramadan. I hope you found this article beneficial insha-allah!

May Allah bless you, and shukran.
