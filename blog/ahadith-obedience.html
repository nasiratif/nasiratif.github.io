29 Muḥarram '46 — Da‘wah

6 Aḥādīth that Call to the Obedience of the Prophet (ﷺ)

The Sunnah (i.e, the way of the Prophet (ﷺ)) contains many general practices of our beloved Prophet (ﷺ), and in it are also narrations that oblige us to obey him (ﷺ). I will be including 6 I've gathered from the book, Follow the Sunnah of the Prophet (ﷺ) by Sheikh al-Albanī (رحمه الله), published by Darussalam:

1.

It was reported on the authority of Abū Hurairah (رضي الله عنه) that the Prophet (ﷺ) said:

"All of my Ummah will enter Paradise except those who refuse."

He was then asked:

"Who would refuse?"

He (ﷺ) said:

"Whoever obeys me will enter Paradise and whosoever disobeys me, refuses [to enter Paradise]." (1)

And Allāh (ﷻ) knows best.

May Allāh bless you, and shukran.

Sources :

1. Ṣaḥīḥ al-Bukhārī
