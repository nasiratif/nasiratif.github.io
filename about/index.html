About this Site

Assalāmu ‘Alaykum wa rahmatullāhi wa barakātuh.

This is an islāmic blog that is ran independently by me, Nāṣir ʿAbd al-Ḥaqq ʿAṭif ibn Salīm ʿAbd al-Malik.

By the grace and permission of Allāh, He (سبحانه و تعالى) has allowed me to create this website to benefit Muslims across the globe, alḥamdulillāh. This site is something that I use to share benefits I come across and write mostly concise articles from that source of benefit. I do my best to restrain from misinformation.

You can also head over to my second website, salafiyyah.net.

Policy

Entire articles from this website can be shared anywhere without modification as long as they link back to the original article. Brief information from the articles can be copied over anywhere with no credit required, as I've made this site for the sake of Allāh alone, to increase the knowledge of the reader, and for them to share it to others.
